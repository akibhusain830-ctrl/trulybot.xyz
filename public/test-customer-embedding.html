How to Embed on Your Website

Step 1: Replace "YOUR_BOT_ID" with your actual bot ID from your Trulybot dashboard.
<script src="https://trulybot.xyz/widget.js" data-bot-id="YOUR_BOT_ID" data-position="right" data-color="#2563eb" async ></script>

Advanced Options

  • data-position="right" - Position: "right" or "left"
  • data-color="#2563eb" - Button color (hex code)
  • data-greeting="Chat" - Button label
  • data-z="2147483000" - Z-index for layering

Troubleshooting

If the widget doesn't appear:
  1. Open browser Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Check the Console tab for error messages
  3. Look for red error messages starting with "[Trulybot widget]"
  4. Verify your bot ID is correct
  5. Ensure the script loads from https://trulybot.xyz/widget.js
